## üìä PAYE (Pay As You Earn) - Individual Income Tax

### Monthly Tax Bands (USD)

| Income Range (USD) | Tax Rate | Deduction | Effective On |
|-------------------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| $0 - $100 | 0% | $0 | Tax-free threshold |
| $100.01 - $300 | 20% | $20 | Low income |
| $300.01 - $1,000 | 25% | $35 | Middle income |
| $1,000.01 - $2,000 | 30% | $85 | Upper middle |
| $2,000.01 - $3,000 | 35% | $185 | High income |
| $3,000.01+ | 40% | $335 | Maximum rate |

### Monthly Tax Bands (ZiG - Zimbabwe Gold)

| Income Range (ZiG) | Tax Rate | Deduction | Effective On |
|-------------------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| Z$0 - Z$2,800 | 0% | Z$0 | Tax-free threshold |
| Z$2,800.01 - Z$8,400 | 20% | Z$560 | Low income |
| Z$8,400.01 - Z$28,000 | 25% | Z$980 | Middle income |
| Z$28,000.01 - Z$56,000 | 30% | Z$2,380 | Upper middle |
| Z$56,000.01 - Z$84,000 | 35% | Z$5,180 | High income |
| Z$84,000.01+ | 40% | Z$9,380 | Maximum rate |

### PAYE Calculation Formula

```
Tax = (Taxable Income √ó Rate) - Deduction
```

**Example (USD):**
- Monthly salary: $1,500
- Tax = ($1,500 √ó 30%) - $85 = $365

## üè¢ Corporate Income Tax

| Tax Type | Rate | Applied To |
|----------|------|------------|
| **Corporate Tax** | 25% | Taxable income |
| **AIDS Levy** | 3% | Tax amount (not income) |
| **Effective Rate** | ~25.75% | Combined |

### Calculation Example

```
Taxable Income:     $100,000
Corporate Tax (25%): $25,000
AIDS Levy (3%):      $750      (3% of $25,000)
Total Tax:          $25,750
```

## üíª Digital Services Withholding Tax (NEW 2026)

| Tax Type | Rate | Effective Date |
|----------|------|----------------|
| **DSWT** | 15% | January 1, 2026 |

### Applicable Services

- Streaming: Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, Apple Music, YouTube Premium
- Cloud Services: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
- Internet: Starlink, Satellite Internet
- Ride-Hailing: Bolt, InDrive, Uber
- E-Commerce: Amazon, eBay, AliExpress
- Software: Adobe, Microsoft 365, Zoom
- Any foreign digital platform payment

### Compliance

- **Withhold:** 15% at source when making payment
- **Remit:** By 10th of following month to ZIMRA
- **Records:** Keep all payment and withholding certificates

## üè• Medical Aid Tax Credit

| Benefit | Rate | Applied To |
|---------|------|------------|
| **Medical Aid Credit** | 50% | Medical aid contributions |

**Important:** This is a **tax credit** (reduces tax), not a deduction (reduces income)

**Example:**
- Medical aid: $200/month
- Tax credit: $100/month (deducted from tax due)

## üìà Tax Calculation Examples

### Example 1: Individual Employee (USD)

```
Monthly Salary:              $2,500
NSSA (3.5%, capped):         $58.33
Medical Aid:                 $200
Pension:                     $125

Taxable Income:              $2,500
PAYE (35% - $185):           $690
Medical Credit (50%):        -$100
AIDS Levy (3% of $690):      $20.70
Total Tax:                   $610.70

Net Pay:                     $1,506.27
```

### Example 2: Corporate Tax

```
Revenue:                     $500,000
Cost of Sales:               $200,000
Operating Expenses:          $150,000
Operating Profit:            $150,000

Non-Deductible (Depreciation): +$20,000
Non-Taxable (Dividends):     -$10,000
Capital Allowances:          -$30,000

Taxable Income:              $130,000
Corporate Tax (25%):         $32,500
AIDS Levy (3%):              $975
Total Tax:                   $33,475

Effective Rate:              25.75%
```

### Example 3: Digital Services Tax (NEW)

```
Netflix Subscription:        $15.99/month
Annual Payment:              $191.88

```

## üéØ Quick Tips

1. **PAYE:** Tax-free threshold is $100 USD or Z$2,800 per month
2. **Corporate Tax:** Don't forget the 3% AIDS Levy on top of 25% tax
3. **Digital Services:** New 15% withholding tax from January 2026
4. **NSSA:** Capped at $700 per person per year
5. **Medical Aid:** 50% tax credit, not deduction
6. **Capital Allowances:** Use special initial (50%) in first year
7. **VAT:** Increased to 15% from January 2025
8. **Compliance:** File and pay on time to avoid 10% penalty
